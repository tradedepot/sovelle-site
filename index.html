Manufacturing Capabilities

Integrated textile production from yarn to finished fabric

Spinning & Yarn Prep

  • Combed and ring-spun cotton processing
  • Custom blends on request (cotton/poly, cotton/modal)
  • Yarn counts from Ne 10s to Ne 60s
  • Quality control at every stage

Weaving & Knitting

  • Air-jet looms for woven fabrics up to 300cm width
  • Circular knitting lines for jersey and interlock
  • Jacquard capabilities for complex patterns
  • Seamless tube knitting for technical applications

Dyeing & Finishing

  • Reactive piece dyeing on jet and soft-flow lines
  • Bleaching and sanforizing
  • Enzyme and soft finishing treatments
  • brushed, peached and anti-microbial finishes

Cut-and-Sew (Select Programs)

  • Bed linen sets and pillowcases
  • Terry towels and bathrobes
  • Table linens and napkins
  • Custom hospitality programs
