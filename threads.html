Caring for Your Investment

With proper care, your Threads by Sovelle linens will become softer and more beautiful with age, lasting for years to come.

Washing

Machine wash in cool water with gentle detergent. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners to preserve the natural cotton fibers.

Drying

Tumble dry on low heat or line dry in shade. Remove while slightly damp for easier ironing and to prevent over-drying.

Ironing

Iron on medium heat while slightly damp for best results. Use steam setting for stubborn wrinkles on percale weaves.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place with good air circulation. Fold loosely or roll to prevent permanent creasing.
